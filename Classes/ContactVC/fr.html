Fraud Reporting

Canada Guaranty encourages lenders to report all cases of mortgage fraud and misrepresentation upon discovery. In an effort to combat this problem, we are committed to sharing information following the review of such cases with our industry partners and stakeholders.

For more information, please visit the Mortgage Fraud Prevention centre or, if you wish to receive additional training on mortgage fraud awareness, please contact your dedicated Canada Guaranty Account Executive.

Collaboration is essential to combating mortgage fraud in Canada. As an industry partner, if you suspect fraud or misrepresentation, we encourage you to notify the Canada Guaranty fraud team via e-mail at: fraudwatch@canadaguaranty.ca
